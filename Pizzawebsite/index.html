MENU

VEG

  • MARGHERITA PIZZA

    A classic and timeless favorite, the Margherita pizza features a thin crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, fresh basil leaves, creamy mozzarella cheese, and a drizzle of olive oil. Simple yet delicious, it's perfect for those who appreciate the beauty of minimalism.

  • VEGGIE SUPREME PIZZA

    Packed with a medley of colorful vegetables, the Veggie Supreme pizza bursts with flavors. The crust is generously spread with tomato sauce, then adorned with toppings such as bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, black olives, juicy tomatoes, and sweetcorn, all covered in gooey melted cheese. A wholesome treat for veggie lovers!

  • SPINACH AND FETA PIZZA

    This gourmet option features a delightful combination of saut√©ed spinach and crumbled feta cheese on a thin crust with a garlic-infused tomato sauce. The earthy taste of spinach complements the tangy feta, providing a unique and delightful culinary experience.

  • BBQ VEGGIE PIZZA

    For those who crave a smoky twist, the BBQ Veggie pizza delivers. The base is layered with barbecue sauce, topped with a mix of grilled vegetables like zucchini, eggplant, red onions, and red bell peppers, then smothered in a blend of mozzarella and cheddar cheese, leaving you with a satisfying, flavorful pizza.

NON VEG

  • PEPPERONI PIZZA

    A timeless classic, Pepperoni pizza is a carnivore's delight. Featuring a traditional tomato sauce, a generous layer of mozzarella cheese, and the star of the show - savory, spicy pepperoni slices. The combination of crispy, spicy, and cheesy makes this a crowd-pleasing favorite.

  • BBQ CHICKEN PIZZA

    The BBQ Chicken pizza offers a delightful fusion of tangy barbecue sauce, tender pieces of grilled chicken, sliced red onions, and a blend of mozzarella and cheddar cheese. The smoky flavors of the barbecue sauce and the succulent chicken create a mouthwatering treat.

  • MEAT FEAST PIZZA

    For the ultimate carnivorous experience, the Meat Feast pizza is a carnivore's dream come true. This hearty delight features a tomato sauce base, layered with an assortment of delicious meat toppings such as pepperoni, Italian sausage, ham, and bacon, all smothered in melted cheese.

  • SEAFOOD DELIGHT PIZZA

    If you're a seafood lover, the Seafood Delight pizza is a must-try. The crust is topped with a creamy garlic sauce, succulent shrimp, tender pieces of crab or lobster, and a sprinkling of fresh herbs. It's a delicious blend of flavors from the sea.
